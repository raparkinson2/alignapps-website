Everything your team needs

One app.
No more chaos.

From RSVP tracking to lineups and payment tracking, ALIGN Sports handles it all so you can focus on playing.

📅

Smart Scheduling

Create games, practices, and events in seconds. Players get instant notifications. Track who's in or out — and set jersey colors for each game.

  • List and calendar views
  • Game check-in / check-out
  • Schedule invites or release now
  • Final scores & game stats
👥

Roster Management

Keep your full roster organized with roles, positions, jersey numbers, and status badges. Invite new players by text or email.

💳

Payment Tracking

Assign and track all payments directly in the app. Log Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, and Cash App manually. Know exactly who's paid and who owes. Payment processing powered by Stripe — coming soon.

💬

Team Chat

Real-time group chat with @mentions, GIF support, and image sharing. Everything in one thread — no more scattered texts.

📊

Stats & Records

Track individual and team stats by sport. Season history, win percentages, and career records — all automatically logged.

🏆

Sport-Specific Lineups

Set lineups right in the app. Not just a generic sports tool — ALIGN Sports understands your sport.

  • Hockey — Lines, goalie tracking, +/−
  • Baseball/Softball — 9/10-player batting orders
  • Soccer — 4-4-2 & diamond formations
  • Basketball — Starting 5 + bench management
  • Lacrosse — Men's & Women's formats
📸

Team Photos

A shared photo gallery for the whole team. Take photos in-app or upload from your camera roll — synced instantly for everyone.

🔔

Push Notifications

Game reminders, chat messages, new lineups, payment updates. Your team stays in the loop — even if they forget to check the app.

📋

Lineup Creation

Build and manage lineups right in the app. No more spreadsheets, group texts, or last-minute scrambles — set your lineup and notify your team instantly.

🔄

Multiple Teams

Play on more than one team? No problem. Create or join multiple teams and switch between them instantly — each with its own roster, schedule, and chat.
